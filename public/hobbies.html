My Hobbies

When I'm not working, I can be found pursuing many other passions.

Parenting: I've got a kid and it's the best and hardest job in the world. They're way more creative and artistically talented than I was at their age. Doing things with them brings me such joy.

Creative activities: It shouldn't come as any surprise that a former art teacher likes to draw and do other crafty and artistic things. I also like to play guitar.

Gardening: My wife is more the planner, picking out various native plants for our house. I'm more like the hired labor, but it is something I enjoy. It gives me a chance to be outside, measure time by the seasons and as someone recently told me: "It's an optimistic pursuit."

Sports: Baseball is my personal favorite, though I'm also a big football fan. I root for my local teams, the Phillies and the Eagles. The mainstream sports are fun, though I also love the drama and narrative of sports, which I can find in many other sports. I used to be way more into cycling and I've been getting more into soccer as I get older.
